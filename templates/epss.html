About EPSS

The Exploit Prediction Scoring System (EPSS) is a data-driven effort for estimating the probability that a vulnerability will be exploited in the wild within 30 days of public disclosure.

  • Score Range: 0.0 to 1.0 (probability of exploitation)
  • Percentile: Ranking relative to all other CVEs (shown as %)
  • Updated: Daily
  • Source: FIRST.org EPSS

EPSS complements CVSS by providing a probability of exploitation, helping prioritize which vulnerabilities to patch first based on real-world threat data.
